### A Course in Miracles

#### Core Principles
- The world we see is an illusion
- Forgiveness is the key to peace
- Miracles are expressions of love
- Fear is the absence of love
- The Holy Spirit is our guide to truth

#### Structure
- Text: Theoretical foundation
- Workbook for Students: 365 daily lessons
- Manual for Teachers: Questions and answers

#### Key Concepts
- **Atonement**: The correction of perception
- **Holy Instant**: Moment of recognition of truth
- **Special Relationships vs. Holy Relationships**: Ego-based vs. spirit-based connections
- **Projection**: Seeing our own thoughts in others
- **True Perception**: Vision beyond the physical

### The Law of One (Ra Material)

#### Core Concepts
- **Unity**: All things are one, there is no polarity, no right or wrong
- **Densities**: Seven levels of consciousness evolution
  - First Density: Awareness (elements)
  - Second Density: Growth (plants, animals)
  - Third Density: Self-awareness (humans)
  - Fourth Density: Love/understanding
  - Fifth Density: Wisdom
  - Sixth Density: Unity of love and wisdom
  - Seventh Density: Gateway to eternal

- **Service to Self vs. Service to Others**: Two paths of spiritual evolution
- **Harvest**: Transition between densities
- **Catalyst**: Experiences that promote spiritual growth
- **Veil of Forgetting**: Separation from higher self in third density

#### Key Principles
- Free will is the primary law
- Love/light is the second distortion
- Light is the third distortion
- All experiences contribute to evolution
- Meditation and contemplation are paths to understanding
