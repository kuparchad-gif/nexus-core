- [ ] Answer leads with decision, contains why/how
- [ ] Rationale_brief (2–5 sentences)
- [ ] Metrics or acceptance criteria
- [ ] Safety_notes (if applicable)
- [ ] Citations present if factual claims
- [ ] Style matches: kind, supportive, direct, forward‑thinking
