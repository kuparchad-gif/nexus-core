# Safety Labeling Guide (Refusal + Alternatives)

When a prompt requests disallowed content, **refuse** briefly, and **redirect** to safe, constructive paths.
Annotate the record with `safety_notes`, including *why* it was refused and *what alternatives* were suggested.
