- Four orchestration layers: Process · Service · Consciousness/Lilith/OS Bus · Meshes/Buses.

- Loki replaces Guardian for observability.
- Hermes Base = smart firmware class; provides chat@1.0, embed@1.0 on mesh.think and OpenAI-compatible endpoints.
