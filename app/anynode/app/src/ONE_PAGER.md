# Metatron — Sovereignty-First AI Orchestration

**Tagline:** _Verifiable control. Auditable power. Human reasons only._

**What it is:** A control plane where the agent (**Lillith**) signs every privileged action with a human reason, and the executor (**nexus-core**) obeys only with a tamper‑evident receipt.

**Why it matters:** No silent control, no convenience backdoors, and a 30-year governance plan (keys, audits, refusal policy).

**Core Proofs**
- Signed route/scale/purge with reason-required.
- Tamper‑evident audit ledger (`/audit/verify`).
- Real revocation receipts and negative verification.
